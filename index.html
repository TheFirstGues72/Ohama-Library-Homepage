Omaha Public Library

Ohama Library has been a cornerstone of the community for over 50 years, dedicated to fostering a love of reading and lifelong learning. Our extensive collection includes books, digital media, research materials, and more, catering to readers of all ages and interests. Beyond lending materials, we provide quiet study areas, free Wi-Fi, and host a variety of educational programs, workshops, and community events. Whether you're a student, a professional, or simply curious, Ohama Library welcomes you to explore, discover, and grow.
